Responsive Redesign

In this project, I chose a Swedish travel agency website (translated below) to redesign responsively. When I first visited Peterbuss, I had no clue what it was for. After examining it, I noted down problems I had with the website.

Chosen Website: Petersbuss

Problems with the website

  • Usability, Learnability, and Memorability

    1. There's no clear text hierarchy. Font size is sometimes too small.

    2. Home page is cluttered with a lot of unsegregated information making navigation hard

    3. The objective of the website (CTA) is not clear.

    4. There are too many unnecessary colors.

    5. The images on the home page are low resolution and have distracting backgrounds

  • Accessibility

    1. No defined language for screen reader.

    2. Missing alt text for images due to which screen reader cannot interpret correctly.

    3. Very low contrast

    4. No page regions to provide navigation functionality to assistive technology users
